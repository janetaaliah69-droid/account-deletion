Account and Data Deletion

At Helping Hand, we deeply value your privacy and data security. We understand that you may, at times, wish to manage your personal information, and we are committed to providing you with a straightforward process to delete your Helping Hand account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account:

Deleting your Helping Hand account is a simple and direct process. Please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the Helping Hand App: Access the app by logging in with your registered email and password as you normally would.
  2. Navigate to the Main Page: After successful login, you will be directed to the main page of the Helping Hand app.
  3. Locate the Delete Account Button: In the top right corner of the main page, you will find the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Click the Button: Tap on the "Delete Account" button.

What Happens to Your Data:

Once you click the "Delete Account" button, your Helping Hand account and all associated data will be completely and permanently deleted.

This includes your personal information, profile details, and any data you generated or stored within the Helping Hand app. All such data will be removed from our systems without any additional retention period.

No data will be retained.

Your privacy is our utmost priority.
